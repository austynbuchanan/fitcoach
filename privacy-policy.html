Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 11, 2026

FitCoach ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the FitCoach mobile application and website.

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us when you create an account and use the app:

  • Email address and password (for account creation)
  • Profile information: name, age, height, weight, and fitness goals
  • Health and fitness data: food logs, calorie intake, workout logs, weight entries, water intake, and daily habits
  • Progress photos (stored locally on your device)
  • Messages sent to your AI coach

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Calculate personalized calorie and nutrition targets
  • Sync your data across devices
  • Provide AI coaching responses tailored to your goals
  • Improve the app and our services

Data Storage

Your account and fitness data is securely stored using Supabase, a trusted cloud database provider. Data is encrypted in transit and at rest. Progress photos are stored only on your device and are never uploaded to our servers.

AI Coach

When you use the AI coach feature, your messages and basic profile information (name, goal) are sent to Anthropic's API to generate responses. These messages are not stored by us beyond your active session. Please review Anthropic's privacy policy at anthropic.com/privacy for more information.

In-App Purchases

FitCoach offers optional Pro subscriptions. Purchase transactions are processed by Google Play or Apple App Store. We do not store your payment information. Subscription management is handled through RevenueCat. Please review their privacy policy at revenuecat.com/privacy.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share data only with trusted service providers who assist in operating the app (Supabase, Anthropic, RevenueCat), all of whom are bound by confidentiality agreements.

Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us at the email below.

Children's Privacy

FitCoach is not intended for users under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Export your data

Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data, including encryption in transit (HTTPS) and at rest. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
